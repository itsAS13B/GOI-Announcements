Apok 08/04/2018 20:24
Based on community feedback we've decided to change the rules to permit the use of the Instant Restart mod by AS13B. The mod itself can be found in the resources page on speedrun.com Instead of: Time will begin upon completion of the loading screen after starting a "New Game" and Modding the game in any way is not allowed The new rules state: Video must include the entire run, from the initial panning motion of the camera and pot animation to the gray end screen and The Instant Restart Mod by AS13B is the only permitted mod. Using any other mod will result in the run getting rejected. Please keep in mind that if you're using the mod and any irregularities that could arguably change the run in any way (such as a strange animation or incorrect respawn position) occur we will still reject the run, so make sure you pay attention. We believe this mod has the potential to make the game more enjoyable to speedrun, especially for those with slower PCs. Happy climbing! (edited)

Hi everyone, Just a quick update on the ccfst situation. I'll try keep this short, Cheating for the world record definitely effected the community more than just a regular cheated run of course. We've decided that ccfst will have an extended ban period which will be 6 months rather than 2 months. For anyone not aware the second time someone is caught cheating it's then a permanent ban, Even after his or anyone elses ban period is over we don't simply accept their runs again. New precautions have been taking into consideration to prevent cheated runs slipping through which we won't go into detail about as we don't want to give anyone a chance to counter these methods although I believe It would be pretty hard to do that with all the steps we'll be taking.

Keronari 01/04/2019 04:32
@everyone Hey everyone, I'm excited to announce a new category for Getting Over It "Golden Snake". This is something that has been done in the past as races and has even been mentioned recently. I am of course talking about the golden pot, The race to 50 wins. There is a bit of a twist to it though, It's not a category of getting 50 wins / black pot to gold but rather riding the snake 50 times. Riding the snake 50 times seems more efficient for a speedrun, rather than having to start new games all the time you only have to start and finish once. Don't worry though we have you covered for anyone that doesn't want to keep track of how many times they have taken the snake. Thanks to @derpidude 🤠 and @as13b we have created an autosplitter with all the splits so you can rely on Livesplit keeping track of your progress. This category will definitely favour runners with consistency so I'm looking forward to see who can come out on top.
